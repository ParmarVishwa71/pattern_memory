HOW TO PLAY

  1. Watch closely: A sequence of tiles will light up and play audio notes.
  2. Repeat the pattern: Click or press key shortcuts to repeat the exact sequence in order.
  3. Level Up: Each successful round adds one extra step to the pattern!
  4. Lives & Timer: Beat the timer! You have 3 Lives (❤️). Timing out or clicking the wrong tile loses 1 Life.
  5. Hint System: Use 💡 HINT up to 3 times per game to replay the current sequence!
  6. Advanced Mode: Successfully completing Level 5 automatically unlocks the 4×4 grid!

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • 1-9 or 0, Q-Y: Tap matching grid tile
  • Spacebar: Start or Pause game
  • H: Use Hint
  • M: Toggle Audio Mute
